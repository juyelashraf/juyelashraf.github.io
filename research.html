Research Interests

Energy Economics Labor Economics International Economics Agricultural Economics Environmental Economics

My research broadly examines how economic policies, trade dynamics, and environmental pressures interact — with a particular focus on developing economies and agricultural systems. I am interested in how energy transitions, labor market forces, and international trade shape economic outcomes at both the macro and micro levels.
